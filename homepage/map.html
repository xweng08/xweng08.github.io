Sunset Park

Multicultural Sunset Park is known for both a booming Chinatown, with noodle shops and dim sum spots, and a diverse Latino community. From a leafy hilltop, the namesake park offers Statue of Liberty views, while pastoral Green-Wood Cemetery is permanent home to famous New Yorkers. On the waterfront, the Industry City warehouse complex houses creative industries and draws locals for casual artisanal eats and spirits.
